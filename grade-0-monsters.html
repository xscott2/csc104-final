in this section i will explain what grade 0 monsters are how they work and what their purpose is in the deck

there are a total of 5 different grade 0 monsters in the basic decks these are

starter

a starter is the first card on the field at the begining of the game. it is picked out and set face down before the game starts by each player.
heal

a heal trigger activates either when you draw it during your trigger check while you are attacking or during the damage check when you opponent is attacking. it grants you 5000 power to a unit and recovery of one damage aslong as you have more damage then your opponent.
stand

the stand trigger is fairly straight forward, it allows a unit on the field to stand once again along with the 5000 power boost.
critical

like the other triggers the critical trigger will only activate under the two coditions mentioned before unless other effects say otherwise. critical trigger will give a power boost to a selected creature aswell as an extra damage while attacking. as a side note this trigger is more effective on your turn not your oponents.
draw

draw trigger does exactly what it says, that is allowing you to draw when this trigger is reviewed. along with the power boost.
