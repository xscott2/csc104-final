in this section i will explain what grade 1 monsters are how they work and what their purpose is in the deck

there are a total of 5 different grade 1 monsters in the basic decks these are

perfect guard a perfect guard is a card that allows one to complete guard and cancel out any damage that would have been done. the cost to activate this is a simple discard.
grade 1 with no effect this is a standard vanilla card with no effect.
grade 1 with an effect morgana is a card included in the basic deck that grants a power boost when she attacks. this can be useful to be able to get through the opponents defenses.
