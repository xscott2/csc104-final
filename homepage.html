lets learn how to play Vanguard





Your a new player wanting to learn to play Cardfight Vanguard but dont understand the rules and mecanics. this site is designed to assist in learning the mecanics of the game in a way young players can understand and not get overwelmed.

This game might be overwelming at first glance but its actually relatively easy to learn and play. but like anything at first it it gets easier the more you play. For example i started playing when the game was released in 2010. and with nothing but a structure deck, I entered a tournement and took 2nd place with nothing but the absolute basic structure deck just released with no modifications to the deck
